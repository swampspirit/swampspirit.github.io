Summary

     We all have different preferences in our lemon bars, from gooey or solid, sweet or tart. Lemon bar recipes are mixed with raspberries and cheesecakes, but my favourite recipe is old fashioned style lemon bars. Nothing fancy, mind you, but hard as a brick with eight lemons in every bar.

Directions

  1. Preheat Oven to 350 Degrees
  2. Press Crust into the Bottom of a Pan
  3. Mix Filling in a pot until it gels
  4. Cook Crust for 30 Minutes
  5. Pour Lemon on Crust
  6. Cook until lemon doesn't wiggle when you wiggle the pan
  7. Don't top with powdered sugar. That is messy and gross

     Enjoy!

*No substitutions. You will be poisoned and die.
