World Maker

Worldbuilding, Character Creation, and Community all in one place!

World Maker tracks information on setting and character for you. Answer more questions to build a complex characters or worlds, or use your questions and filters to find other writers to talk with!

You can answer thousands of in depth questions help you build a world's landscape and culture, and tracks all your notes for you. Even more questions help you create and track complex characters and their relationships.

Share worlds with other writers, or keep a private profile for your own notes. Meet other writers for collaboration, writing workshops, or just making new friends.

World Maker also has image galleries, character designers, and tons of other fun options.
