FAQ
What do you need to know about?

Account Features

My friend has more questions than me. Why?

Check to see if you've hidden any of your questions. Otherwise, your friend probably has an expansion you don't. You can check which sets they have on their profile.

Can I set up more than one world?

You can make one world and up to three cultures on a free account, or unlock a paid account.

Why can't I link world to my friend's account?

You can only link worlds if you have an open world slot. This is to make sure people aren't using multiple accounts to build one coherent world. We are sorry for the trouble this causes and are looking for ways to build collaboration without letting certain users abuse it.

I don't show up as online

Look at your settings and make sure you don't have "Hide Me" checked.

My ID picture keeps resetting to the default picture. I don't want to spend my life as an apple!

Never fear! Just make sure you hit 'confirm upload' before 'save changes'. If that isn't working, double check that your image is the right size and type.

Where's my account confirmation email?

Check your spam folder. If it's not there, double check your email and use this link to resend it.

Community

How do I block or report a user?

Both buttons should show up on the lower right of their personal page. If you report, please make sure to tell us your reason.

Why can't I read the horror or mature expansion on other people's profiles?

These are automatically blocked so you won't stumble across them on accident. If you're over 18, you can turn off this filter in your settings.

Can I report a specific post?

Reports are by user, but please link us to the specific post in your report. If it was deleted, tell us anyway and we can find it on archives.

New Features

When will I see the RP feature?

The on site RP feature is in progress. We're hoping to have it ready by Fall, 2015!

Will there be an expansion on [x]?

You can find a list of current and coming expansions here. If there's an expansion you'd like to see, you can tell us about it on our Contact Us page!

What happened to your plans to team up with Amazon for publishing?

Unfortunately, this announcement was not made by a member of our staff, but by a fake blog. We have many plans to keep our company growing and changing, but have no interest to get involved with publishing.

My account doesn't show the latest update.

You need to log out and log back in to let your account refresh.
